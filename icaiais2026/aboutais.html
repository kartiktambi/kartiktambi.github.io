AI Innovation Summit –
AIS 2026

The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI), established under the Chartered Accountants Act, 1949, is the world’s largest professional body of Chartered Accountants, functioning under the administrative oversight of the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India. With a legacy of excellence and a commitment to nation-building, ICAI continues to lead the profession into the future by embracing innovation and emerging technologies.

AI Innovation Summit (AIS) 2026, scheduled on 26th & 27th June 2026 at Bharat Mandapam, New Delhi, is a flagship global platform that brings together the brightest minds in finance, technology, and governance. This two-day summit is designed to explore how Artificial Intelligence is redefining the future of the accounting profession and beyond.

With the theme “Transforming Accounting, Audit, Tax, and Governance”, AIS 2026 will showcase how AI is revolutionizing financial ecosystems—enabling smarter decision-making, enhancing compliance, driving efficiency, and unlocking new strategic possibilities. As businesses adapt to rapid technological shifts and evolving regulatory frameworks, this summit will serve as a catalyst for innovation and transformation.

Participants will experience a dynamic blend of insightful keynotes, high-impact panel discussions, real-world use cases, and live demonstrations, delivered by industry leaders, global experts, policymakers, and innovators. The summit will also host the much-awaited AI Hackathon Grand Finale, highlighting cutting-edge solutions developed by emerging talent.
